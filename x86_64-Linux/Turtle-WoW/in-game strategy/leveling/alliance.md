## Quick Tips for Level 26 Warlock

1.  **Pet Choice:** Use **Voidwalker** for safe solo grinding. Switch to **Succubus** for Crowd Control (2+ mobs) or PvP.
2.  **Key Ability:** You now have **Drain Life (Rank 2)** ‚Äì great for sustainability.
3.  **Dungeon Alert:** Start looking for **Scarlet Monastery** groups at **Level 28**. The Graveyard wing drops the **Gravestone Scepter**, a fantastic wand.
4.  **Hearthstone:** Set it to **Southshore** (Hillsbrad) or **Darkshire** (Duskwood) depending on your chosen zone.

## üíé Core Tips for the 40-60 Grind

*   **Mount Priority:** Your **Level 40 mount** is the #1 priority for speed. Save gold for it. Start your **Level 60 Epic Mount (Dreadsteed)** quest chain around level 52-54.
*   **Pet Strategy:** **Succubus** becomes your default questing pet for **Seduction** (CC). Switch to **Voidwalker** for elite mobs, dangerous areas, or when you need a sturdy tank. The **Felhunter** is excellent in Felwood/Azshara and for PvP.
*   **Optimal Rotation:** Keep **Corruption** and **Curse of Agony** active on all targets. Use **Shadow Bolt** as your main filler. **Drain Life** is a powerful emergency heal. **Fear** (but be mindful of adds).
*   **Dungeon Loot Goals:**
    *   **Zul'Farrak:** Aim for the **Hypnotic Blade** (dagger).
    *   **Blackrock Depths / Lower Blackrock Spire:** Excellent for pre-raid gear.
    *   **Endgame:** **Stratholme** and **Scholomance** are your final dungeons for Best-in-Slot (BiS) items before raiding.
*   **Profession Advantage:** If you have **Skinning, Herbalism, or Mining**, the materials in the 40-60 zones are highly valuable. They are the best way to fund your epic mount and consumables.
