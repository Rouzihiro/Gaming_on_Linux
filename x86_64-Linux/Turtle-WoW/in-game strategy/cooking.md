## üìà Leveling Guide

### Apprentice: Skill 1 to 50
*   **Action:** Make about **55x** of one recipe.
*   **Notes:** Buy **Soothing Spices**, **Hot Spices**, and **Refreshing Spring Water** from vendors near your trainer (not the Auction House).

**Recommended Recipes:**
| Food | Ingredients | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Charred Wolf Meat | Stringy Wolf Meat | Trainer |
| Spiced Wolf Meat (Season of Discovery) | Stringy Wolf Meat | Trainer at skill 10 |

**Faction Alternatives:**
*   **Horde:** **Crispy Bat Wing** (Meaty Bat Wing). Recipe sold by Abigail Shiel (Tirisfal Glades).
*   **Horde:** **Scorpid Surprise** (Scorpid Stinger). Recipe sold by Grimtak (Durotar).
*   **Alliance:** **Kaldorei Spider Kabob** (Small Spider Leg). Recipe from "Recipe of the Kaldorei" quest (Zarrin, Teldrassil).

### Journeyman: Skill 50 to 100
*   **Prerequisite:** Reach level 10, Cooking 50. Learn **Journeyman Cooking** from your trainer.
*   **Action:** Make about **55x** of one recipe. **Smoked Bear Meat** turns yellow sooner, so prioritize it if you have multiple meats.

**Recommended Recipes:**
| Food | Ingredients | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Smoked Bear Meat | Bear Meat | Trainer |
| Boiled Clams | Clam Meat, Refreshing Spring Water | Trainer |
| Coyote Steak | Coyote Meat | Trainer |
| Longjaw Mud Snapper | Raw Longjaw Mud Snapper | Vendors |
| Rainbow Fin Albacore | Raw Rainbow Fin Albacore | Vendors |
| Strider Stew | Strider Meat, Shiny Red Apple | Tari'qa (Horde) / "Easy Strider Living" quest (Alliance) |

### Journeyman (cont.): Skill 100 to 130
*   **Action:** Make about **30-40x** of one recipe.

**Recommended Recipes:**
| Food | Ingredients | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Crab Cake | Crawler Meat | Trainer |
| Dry Pork Ribs | Boar Ribs | Trainer |
| Bristle Whisker Catfish | Raw Bristle Whisker Catfish | Vendors |
| Cooked Crab Claw | Crawler Claw | Kendor Kabonka (Alliance only) |

### Expert: Skill 130 to 225
*   **Prerequisite:** Reach level 20, Cooking 125. Buy **Expert Cookbook** from:
    *   **Horde:** Wulan (Shadowprey Village, Desolace).
    *   **Alliance:** Shandrina (Ashenvale).

**Skill 130 - 175:**
*   **Primary Recipe:** **Curiously Tasty Omelet** (Raptor Egg). Recipe sold by Kendor Kabonka (Stormwind) and Keena (Arathi Highlands).
*   **Alternatives:** **Bristle Whisker Catfish** or **Hot Lion Chops** (Horde, recipe from Zargh in The Barrens).

**Skill 175 - 225:**
*   **Primary Recipe:** **Roast Raptor** (Raptor Flesh). Recipe sold by Hammon Karwn and Keena (Arathi Highlands).
*   **Alternatives:** **Soothing Turtle Bisque** (quest reward from Hillsbrad Foothills) or **Mithril Headed Trout** (vendor recipe).

### Artisan: Skill 225 to 300
*   **Prerequisite:** Reach level 35, Cooking 225. Complete the **"Clamlette Surprise"** quest chain starting from your faction's trainer (Zamja in Orgrimmar / Daryl Riknussun in Ironforge), leading to Dirge Quikcleave in Gadgetzan (Tanaris).

**Skill 225 - 275:**
*   **Action:** Make about **65x** of one recipe (can push to 285 with cheap materials).
*   **Key Vendor in Tanaris:** Visit **Gikkix** and buy all his recipes.

**Recommended Recipes:**
| Food | Ingredients | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Undermine Clam Chowder | Giant Egg, Ice Cold Milk | Jabbey (Tanaris) |
| Spotted Yellowtail | Spotted Yellowtail | Gikkix (Tanaris) |
| Monster Omelet | Giant Egg | Himmik (Winterspring) / Malygen (Felwood-Alliance) / Bale (Felwood-Horde) |
| Tender Wolf Steak | Tender Wolf Meat | Dirge Quikcleave (Tanaris) |
| Filet of Redgill | Raw Redgill | Kelsey Yance (Booty Bay) |

**Skill 275 - 300:**
*   **Action:** Make about **35x** of one recipe.
*   **Primary Recipes:** **Nightfin Soup** (Raw Nightfin Snapper) or **Poached Sunscale Salmon** (Raw Sunscale Salmon). Both recipes sold by **Gikkix (Tanaris)**.
*   **Alternative:** **Smoked Desert Dumplings** (15x Sandworm Meat). Requires **Cooking 285** and completing two quests in Silithus.
